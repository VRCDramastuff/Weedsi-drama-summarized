## Common-Denominator Read

The core strong point holds: Orion posted "confirmed" and "undeniable and proven" without having Weedsi's counter-evidence in front of him. The DM sequence is unambiguous. The unidentified staff member's statement that Elk claimed "concrete proof" but "never showed us" independently corroborates that the decision-making process inside VIPS was not evidence-driven. DOMObeef's 1/31 "proven to be false" statement, and Gaape's real-time investigation in VIPS staff chat, further establish that counter-evidence existed and was being circulated while the official position was crystallizing.

What the evidence does not support is a clean exoneration. Weedsi's own statement as Milk #6666 (submitted to a mod in a different group) admits she did have a VR handjob with noot, believing him to be 20/21, she was deceived, but the interaction itself was real. The cuddle incident has genuinely contradictory accounts. Weedsi's own VRChat history in the archive shows she was centrally involved in drama across KushCookies, RP Verified, and now The VIPs, this is the third consecutive major group from which she was banned or exiled. "Everyone lied about me" grows less plausible the longer the list, and Kareru's "constant daily drama" characterization comes from someone otherwise sympathetic to Weedsi. The Glitch-started-it framing is plausible but not verifiable within this episode's scope.

The Blairs unban is real pattern evidence that Elk ran the group by personal discretion over staff process. That is relevant to whether his decision on Weedsi was principled or arbitrary. But it does not prove the pedo accusation was fabricated, only that the process that produced it was compromised.
