## Common-Denominator Read

Across this episode, Bean is not only on the receiving end of mistreatment, she is also an active moderator who bans users for territorial/romantic reasons (POison_ivyy), removes a supportive ally without explanation and threatens a blacklist when they push back (djakaspinner), talks trash about a person who then circulates a video about her and then presents the circulation as unprovoked persecution (Coda/16.png), and is accused by a server owner who took real action on her behalf of manipulating and gaslighting him (Alex/4.png). The Shclampywamps complaint (1.png), preserved in her own archive, unrebutted, adds a third-party data point about her staff conduct. "Better off dead in a ditch" from THECRIPPLED is genuine abuse; calling it out is warranted. But the folder's implicit argument, that Bean is consistently the wronged party and everyone else is acting in bad faith, is undercut by her own admissions, by Alex's account from someone who demonstrably supported her, and by the pattern of short-lived alliances ending in conflict. The "rejected me so they turned on me" frame (applied here to Little-fluff) appears repeatedly enough across the archive to be a structural default rather than a documented pattern.
