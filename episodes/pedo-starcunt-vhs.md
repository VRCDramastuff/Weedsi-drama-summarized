## TL;DR

This episode documents Weedsi's campaign against starlight0011, a separate VRChat user she calls "starcunt," whom she accuses of spreading pedophile rumors and coordinating with Glitch to ruin her reputation. The central claim (that Starlight circulated a pedo accusation) is almost entirely Weedsi's own assertion: the one available audio clip of Starlight makes a gold-digger accusation, not a pedo one, and no recording of Starlight actually making the pedo claim is in evidence. The only live confrontation (0406.mp4) captures Starlight briefly denying knowing Weedsi and counter-claiming Weedsi has been trying to get her banned across multiple groups; Weedsi's interpreting her block as a confession of guilt is not the only available read. The crash/DDoS allegation is entirely second-hand via a witness (Boosted) with his own credibility issues. What is well-documented from Weedsi's own material is an aggressive pattern of ban campaigns against people she identifies as enemies, corroborated by her own screenshots and confirmed by a VIPS moderator. The episode also contains no admission of minor contact; Weedsi's narration is entirely defensive, claiming the relevant person was age-verified and lied about being 16.

## Common-denominator read

Weedsi is the connecting thread across every part of this episode: she assembled the archive, supplies almost all the framing, and the most independently corroborated facts about her own behavior, calling for bans of potentially innocent users, framing Starlight's ban from KushCookies as vindication when it was for unrelated conduct, confronting someone in a public instance who then immediately blocks her, sit uncomfortably alongside her victimhood narrative. The counter-claim most supported by independent sources is not the pedo accusation against Starlight but Starlight's claim (corroborated by aslay and by Weedsi's own messages) that Weedsi ran a sustained ban campaign across multiple groups against her. Starlight's blocking during the confrontation is the main piece of "evidence" Weedsi offers, but public confrontations in social VRChat instances are not courts; blocking a hostile person does not indicate guilt. The episode does not disprove that Starlight spread pedo rumors, but it doesn't prove it either; what it demonstrates clearly is Weedsi's aggressive and documented effort to remove Starlight from every shared space, with at least one case of calling for a ban on the wrong person.
