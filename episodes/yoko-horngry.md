## What happened (detailed, chronological)

### Background: Outer hands Horngry to Yoko

At some point prior to September 2025, Outer (Outerspaced) transferred operational control of Horngry, an 18+ VRChat group, to Yoko, with the explicit agreement that "she's the boss." The VRChat group listing (VRCX_glC9vbfESR.png) still shows Outer's account ("outerspaced-") as the associated name, because VRChat group ownership was not necessarily transferred, only operational authority. Weedsi would later use the presence of Outer's name on the group listing to claim a stake in policing Horngry's standards.

### September 29, 2025: The Gate Incident

Weedsi was present in Horngry around 2:20 AM on September 29, 2025. She observed what she characterized as a dysfunction at the entrance gate: ten or more people waiting to get in for over ten minutes, multiple staff members distracted or inattentive ("2 or 3 of the staff werent paying attention"), a person named kordzy who "couldnt hear anyone" due to noise, and three or more non-staff people at the gate making noise.

Weedsi says she made a verbal suggestion, "it would probably be easier if you guys didnt have anyone at the gate talking so people can hear", and that a staff member named mewtew responded "i/we dont give a fuck" and then "started lying and screaming." She adds that someone attempted to report a person for using a racial slur (OCR: "the hard r") and that gate staff laughed at him instead of acting. When Weedsi subsequently tried to re-enter the instance, she says staff were "being childish and petty not letting me in." She also reports that a user she identifies as "kkkkkkkkk2 or something" had let a 14-year-old into the group: the minor was apparently looking for k2, and k2 had given a birth year of 2000 (age 21 in 2025) but later 2004 (age 21 in 2025, wait, 2004 would be 21, but Weedsi writes "said their bday was 21 2000 then they said 2004," suggesting the minor's age was inconsistent and the person admitted to being under age). Weedsi began recording on her device.

By approximately 2:20-2:35 AM, Weedsi had sent Yoko a voice message and a text message from her RAVE account: "this is actually so sad to me. outer said he left horngry to you and if that is the case, you dont deserve this shit." She attached what the screenshot shows as a voice message OGG file. At 2:35 AM she wrote about the potential minor entry, identifying k2 by the long repeated-letter handle.

She told Yoko she would DM the video from her main account (m.ourn) and explained the full sequence of events.

Around 6:49 AM (a several-hour gap in the visible conversation, probably after Weedsi slept or paused), she sent a fuller text account of what happened, naming mewtew and the "we dont give a fuck" response.
