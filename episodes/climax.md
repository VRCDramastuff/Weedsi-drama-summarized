## TL;DR

Weedsi (as Noodle/Niblz) was a mod/staff at "Climax," a VRChat ERP social instance with an age-verification gate and private back-rooms (BClub). Late on March 26, 2025 she reported a group in a private room repeatedly unlocking the door, vulgar conversation, and a rape joke she says was directed at minors. The senior admin Cyber banned her the same night after a Discord argument; the owner Gunner overruled the ban. The folder's headline framing, "pedo behavior," "diddy parties," and "minors let in," is substantially unsupported by the actual screenshot content: no minor is confirmed present, the "about minors" characterization is Weedsi's interpretive add-on to a joke that does not mention minors, and the only documented events are a mod conflict and a staffing complaint. Two independent people within Weedsi's own curated screenshots flag her conduct, not just Cyber's, as the problem.
