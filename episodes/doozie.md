## TL;DR

Doozie (later renamed Waffle), a former KushCookies mod, sent a written Discord confession on 2025-03-13 admitting he lied about Weedsi in KushCookies after being demodded, attributing his behavior to jealousy over her relationship with Neb and to being bipolar. That confession is genuine, voluntary, and unambiguous, it is one of the strongest pieces of direct evidence in this archive. However, the confession specifies no particular lie he told: the specific content (e.g., nude-sharing rumors) is supplied only by Weedsi via filenames and folder placement, not by Doozie himself. Three days after confessing, Doozie backtracked, threatened legal action, and accused someone in Weedsi's group of calling him a pedo, a charge Weedsi neither denied nor repudiated, instead calling it "karma." By June-July 2025, Waffle was circling back through mutual contacts (Outer, Yoko, Neb) before approaching Weedsi directly with an apology that rapidly pivoted to an offer of dirt on Outer. Neither party looks clean across the full arc.

## Common-Denominator Read

The written confession is real and rare, Doozie explicitly admitted lying to hurt Weedsi, and there is no ambiguity in his words. That said, the episode-as-Weedsi-frames-it asks us to accept more than the confession actually contains: the specific rumors (nude-sharing), the causal chain to the video, and the folder-placed 3.png are all her editorial additions, not Doozie's admissions. More tellingly, when Doozie asked on 3/16 whether someone in her group was spreading pedo accusations against him, Weedsi's response was "karmas a bitch", deploying unverified rumor-by-association as deserved punishment, which is structurally identical to what she condemns Glitch and Starlight for doing to her. Outer told her directly that the nude-sharing rumor was coming from "YOUR group," not Glitch's. Weedsi is a skilled victim-narrator and Doozie genuinely wronged her; both things are true simultaneously. The pattern across this archive, long enemy lists, "I have LITERALLY never done a single fucking thing," selective forwarding, is consistent here.
