## Method & Caveats

This is a one-sided archive. Weedsi collected, labeled, captioned, and sequenced every piece of evidence here. Folder titles are her spin, not neutral description; many have been verified against the raw OCR and found to significantly overstate or directly contradict the underlying screenshot content. **Active distrust of framing is required throughout.**

**Evidence tiers used here, ranked by weight:**

1. **Admission**, the person says it about themselves (strongest)
2. **Adversary's own words**, an opponent says something that hurts their own case
3. **Neutral third party**, someone with no stake in the dispute corroborates
4. **Her-word-only**, Weedsi's assertion, no corroboration
5. **Caption-overstated / contradicted**, caption claims more than the underlying screenshot shows, or is flatly contradicted by it (weakest; counts against her credibility)

Weedsi is the common denominator across every group, every relationship, and every conflict documented here. That structural fact is itself evidence. "Everyone is lying about me" gets harder to sustain the longer the list grows.
